HOTEL

A hotel is a place that offers accommodation for a brief duration. A restaurant is a place that serves food and beverages to its customers. Certain hotels offer meals as part of their accommodation. Food is usually served and consumed on premises However, some restaurants provide take-away or food delivery service.

Room Dining

Room service or in-room dining is a hotel service enabling guests to choose items of food and drink for delivery to their hotel room for consumption. Room service is organized as a subdivision within the food and beverage department of high-end hotel and resort properties.
